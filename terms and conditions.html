Privacy Policy & Terms and Conditions

Introduction

Welcome to the Bank Locker Management System. This Privacy Policy and Terms and Conditions outlines the guidelines governing the use of our locker services, data handling, and security measures.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. We ensure that all personal information provided during locker registration and use of services is secure and confidential. This information includes, but is not limited to, your name, address, phone number, and locker details.

1. Data Collection

We collect personal data required for locker management and ensure that the data is only used for its intended purpose.

2. Data Usage

We use your data strictly for service purposes such as locker registration, key management, and providing secure access to lockers.

3. Data Security

We use encryption and other security methods to ensure your data is protected against unauthorized access. However, we cannot guarantee complete data security, and we encourage customers to follow safe practices in managing their access credentials.

Terms and Conditions

1. Eligibility

The use of our Bank Locker Management System is available to customers who hold an account with our bank. By accessing the system, you agree to comply with all the terms outlined here.

2. Locker Allocation

Lockers are allocated on a first-come, first-served basis. Customers must provide valid identification documents to verify their identity.

3. Payment of Locker Fees

Locker fees are charged annually. Non-payment of fees may result in the suspension or termination of locker access.

4. Use of Locker

  • Lockers must be used only for the storage of valuables, not for illegal or hazardous items.
  • The bank holds no responsibility for the contents of the locker beyond the terms of the locker agreement.

5. Key and Access

Customers are responsible for the safe-keeping of their locker keys. In case of loss, the customer must report it immediately, and charges for a new key may apply.

Liability

The bank is not liable for any loss or damage to the items stored in the locker due to natural disasters, theft, or other unforeseen circumstances.

Amendments

We reserve the right to amend these terms and policies from time to time. Customers will be notified of any major changes via email or official communication channels.
